    Minecraft. A simplistic game, consisting of placing blocks down, breaking blocks, and letting your creativity flow. If yo have played before, you may have noticed some small details that tell a story, lore, if you will. For example, broken nether portals are scattered around the world, hinting that there were people before you who went to the hellish place centuries ago. But if you have been playing for a while, you may have noticed one hidden detail that is always right in front of your nose, and most of the time can go undesovered.
